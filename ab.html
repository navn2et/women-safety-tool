A community-based reporting and monitoring tool for women's safety in universities.

Little Women

In recent years, ensuring the safety and security of women in educational institutions, particularly colleges and universities, has become a paramount concern. Reports of harassment, assaults, and other safety-related incidents... have underscored the need for proactive measures to protect female students. This abstract outlines the key features and components of a Community-Based Reporting Monitoring Tool designed to enhance women's safety on college campuses.
